Hackathon Details

Event Overview

Hackathon hosted at the Stellenbosch campus. See complete schedule, rules, prizes, and project ideas below.

Theme: Applied tech build with hands-on project development

Key Information

  • ▶ Date: 11 October 2025
  • ▶ Time: 08:00 – 20:00 (12 hours)
  • ▶ Venue: Belgium Campus — Stellenbosch campus
  • ▶ Eligibility: Only Stellenbosch students
  • ▶ Format: Teams of 3

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 3.
  • AI may not be used.
  • Moderators will ensure fair play.
  • Technolab resources may be used.

Prizes & Awards

  • 1st Place: Top project prize
  • 2nd Place: Second place prize
  • 3rd Place: Third place prize
  • Recognition: All participants receive certificates

Detailed Schedule

Morning Session

  • ▶ 08:00 – 08:10: Welcoming
  • ▶ 08:10 – 08:40: Topic explanation & idealization (Explaining idealization, giving examples)
  • ▶ 08:40 – 08:50: Team selection
  • ▶ 08:50 – 09:30: Work on project (Preferably planning)
  • ▶ 09:30 – 10:00: VR presentation (Either Philip/Nick can hopefully present)
  • ▶ 10:00 – 11:00: IoT Arduino explanation
  • ▶ 11:00 – 12:30: Development time

Afternoon Session

  • ▶ 12:30 – 13:30: Lunch break
  • ▶ 13:30 – 14:00: Presentation on Drones (Provided by Philip or Nick ideally)
  • ▶ 14:00 – 16:00: Development time
  • ▶ 16:00 – 16:30: Implementation phase
  • ▶ 16:30 – 19:30: Final implementation
  • ▶ 19:30 – 20:00: Presentations; prizes and winners announced

Available Technologies & Resources

Students will have access to cutting-edge technologies and tools to bring their innovative ideas to life:

🤖 IoT & Hardware Development

  • Arduino Boards: Various Arduino models for sensor integration and automation
  • Raspberry Pi: Single-board computers for complex IoT solutions
  • Sensors: Motion, temperature, humidity, light, and proximity sensors
  • Actuators: Motors, servos, relays, and LED systems
  • Prototyping Kits: Breadboards, jumper wires, and electronic components

🚁 Drone Technology

  • Drone Kits: Build and program custom drone solutions
  • Flight Controllers: Advanced control systems for autonomous flight
  • Camera Systems: HD cameras for aerial imaging and computer vision
  • GPS Modules: Navigation and positioning systems

🖨️ 3D Printing & Manufacturing

  • 3D Printers: FDM and resin printers for rapid prototyping
  • Laser Cutters: Precision cutting for custom enclosures and parts
  • CAD Software: Design tools for 3D modeling and engineering
  • Materials: Various filaments, resins, and cutting materials

🎮 VR & Augmented Reality

  • VR Headsets: Immersive virtual reality experiences
  • AR Development Kits: Augmented reality application development
  • Motion Controllers: Interactive input devices for VR/AR
  • Development Platforms: Unity, Unreal Engine, and AR frameworks

💻 Software & Development

  • Programming Languages: Python, C++, JavaScript, and more
  • AI/ML Libraries: TensorFlow, PyTorch, and computer vision tools
  • Cloud Services: AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud integration
  • Mobile Development: Android and iOS app development tools
