Event Overview

Hackathon hosted at the Stellenbosch campus. See schedule, rules and prizes below. Full brief available for download.

Download Full Brief (DOCX)

Key Information

  • Theme: Applied tech build (see idea topics below)
  • Date/Time: 13 September, 08:00 – 20:00
  • Venue: Belgium Campus — Stellenbosch campus
  • Eligibility: Only Stellenbosch students
  • Prizes: Top 3 projects receive prizes; Judges' favourite extra prize; Prize for most "out‑of‑syllabus" tools

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 3–5 or solo allowed.
  • AI use restricted to dedicated research time or if building your own AI component.
  • Moderators will ensure fair play.
  • Technolab resources may be used.

Schedule

  • 08:00 – 08:10: Welcoming
  • 08:10 – 08:40: Topic explanation & idealization
  • 08:40 – 08:50: Team selection
  • 08:50 – 10:00: Project planning
  • 10:00 – 11:00: VR presentation
  • 11:00 – 12:30: 3D printing / laser cutting time
  • 12:30 – 13:30: Lunch break
  • 13:30 – 14:00: Drones presentation
  • 14:00 – 16:00: Development time
  • 16:00 – 16:30: Implementation phase
  • 16:30 – 19:30: Final implementation
  • 19:30 – 20:00: Presentations; prizes and winners announced
